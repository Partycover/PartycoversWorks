Older Version Videos

There's tons of videos of Older Versions! There's hundreds of playthroughs out there, but realistically, it would be a nightmare to watch a start-to-end playthrough for each and every version just to find all the differences. An approach some people, such as Kris, who also makes videos on Older Versions, is to start from Version 1, then continue to Version 2 when that's done and so on until all the way up to the current version.

I would recommend checking out Kris's YouTube channel if you're interested in such a playthrough.
