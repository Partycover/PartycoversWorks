Grand Dream City

Grand Dream City is the capital city and largest city in the Aevium Region. It is located between the two sister islands that the Terrial Region is composed of, bolstering a population of 346,932 at the start of the story. It is divided into several districts, including the Judicial, Scholar, Dream, Hope and Residential districts along with Pokestar Studio's.

Grand Dream City was first proposed over 40 years ago not long before Storm-9 in the former capital, Hiyoshi City during a fair before they were stolen by Thomas Blakeory Sr.
