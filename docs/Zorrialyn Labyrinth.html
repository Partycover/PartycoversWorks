Zorrialyn Labyrinth

Zorrialyn Labyrinth is a maze within the Zorrialyn desert containing various items and treasures, such as crests. The Labyrinth has the unique property that it will expel whoever is within it after a certain amount of steps within it. This step count is reset if you move between floors. There are 600 steps available on the first floor, 850 on the second, 650 on the third and 450 on the fourth.

The Labyrinth provides access to Castle Zygara. According to the Archaeoligist in Sahilla Village, the labyrinth was created when the his castle caved into the ground. A common belief the Archaeoligst shares is the curse is a result of King Ieisel interrupting himself in the creation of a Confagrigus crest; Ieisel wishes to be free but the Garufan Curse repels people.
