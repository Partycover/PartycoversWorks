Voidal Chasm

Voidal Chasm is a chasm and mystery dungeon located in the Badlands, North East of Sashila Village. Voidal Chasm's first three layers remain static whereas subsequent change in a formation each time a person enters, thus making it impossible to create maps or plan a route beforehand.

Voidal Chasm exhibits behaviour synonymous to a parasite; the chasm actively drains the life force of people within it to sustain itself. This behaviour can lead to sickness within people, with those in for extended periods of time losing their energy, their personality and their memories. Despite this the chasm will not cause death and will keep those lost within it alive, it can presumed Voidal Chasm has anti-aging properties.

Voidal Chasm is visited often from people outside the Badlands due to the ease in mining Ether from there.
