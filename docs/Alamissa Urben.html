Alamissa Urben

Alamissa, "the city of happiness", is a ruined city in the Badlands East of Sashilla Village. Alamissa's construction started shortly after the Storm-9 after those living in the area realised nobody would come to their aid. Alamissa's construction was described as Ryland as "hard" with how much destruction "Storm-9" had brung. With the efforts of William Grevillea and Herald Jenkel, the city and the Badlands began to grow, with oases starting to appear and a Pokemon population returning.

Years prior to the events of the story Team Xen, lead by Xen Admin Cassandra, launched an invasion of Alamissa Urben with the intent to execute the champion of the Aevium League, Celine. During the invasion, the opening of the ground beneath Alamissa caused Cassandra to order an immediate retreat, believing the crevice would finish Alamissa up.

Resident's Adam, Ryland, Alexandra and Damien brought survivors to the "Ruined City", until Flora Grevillea, William Grevillea's daughter restored the land West of the Ruined City, leading to the construction and relocation to Sashila Village.
