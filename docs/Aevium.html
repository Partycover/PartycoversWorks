Aevium Region

The Aevium region is an archipelago region in close proximity to Kalos and Galar and exactly 9 Hours East of the Miera Region. There are four sub-regions which make up Aevium, these being the Floria, Terajuma, Terrial and Artrica Regions. Only three of these four regions can be explored in the current version of the game.

Demographics

Aevium bolsters a considerable population, with over 346,932 people living in Grand Dream City alone. The vast majority of Aevium's residents are presumably immigrants from the Miera Region, with some individuals, such as Kenneth, immigrating following the calamity.

History

Aevium's exact origins are uncertain, however it contains the sites of various long-fallen kingdoms, such as the Kingdom of Ghorova. Aevium also includes relics of the Old Earth, such as The Archive, The Third Layer and Eclysia Pyramid.

Approximately 40 years prior to the events of the game, Aevium was largely a single landmass. When Storm-9, the calamity, occured, the region was split into the several landmasses it is today.

Climate

Aevium's climate varies considerably across each of it's four regions with the Artrica region being the coldest and the Terajuma and Badlands, one of the islands that compose the Terrial Region, presumably being the hottest.

Floria Region

The Floria Region's climate is very varied, with the southern parts of the region generally appearing warm whereas northernmost parts of the island generally being cold as evidenced in Akuwa Town and Evergreen Island. The topology of the region is uncertain, but it can be assumed topology and the mountainous enviroment may contribute to Floria Region's varied climate.

Terajuma Region

The Terajuma Region contains a tropical climate, with rain being typical. Whilst snow is rare on Terajuma, it can be seen in areas of higher altitude, such as the summit of Mt Terajuma. Terajuma features a volcano at it's Northernmost Island.

Terrial Region

The Terrial Region's climate is largely varied, with the northernmost point of the island generally appearing to be cooler than the southernmost points.

Badlands

The Badlands climate is generally arid, consisting of a vast desert stretching across most of the island. Whilst the exact cause of the Badlands arid climate is unknown, this can potentially be explained by rainshadow effect and by Mt Ruin, a volcano sitting on Reldin Island.
